Who We Are

Welcome to Fewa Kokusai Educational Consultancy Pvt. Ltd, a premier education consultancy offering language courses, preparation, and university placement services in Japan.Registered and approved by the Government of Nepal, Fewa was established by dedicated professionals to assist aspiring students.

We offer a variety of Japanese language and culture courses taught by native instructors. Our students benefit from extensive learning resources, including daily audio-visual materials, interactive tutorials, and engaging activities. We provide intensive test preparation for exams like JLPT, NAT, and TOPJ, featuring regular mock tests and rigorous practice sessions. In addition, we help students enroll in colleges and universities across Japan, guiding them through applications for CSC, Provincial, and University Scholarships. We support both English and Japanese language studies, assist with visa documentation, and offer scholarships for eligible students. Once in Japan, we ensure our students are well-supported academically and personally, maintaining regular contact and visits to partner schools. We are proud to be associated with Ryugakusai Kabushiki Kaisha, a renowned Japanese language school. This esteemed institution not only excels in language education but also introduces international students from over 30 countries to the Japanese language and culture. Through our partnership, we provide students with the opportunity to immerse themselves in high-quality language programs and gain a deeper understanding of Japan. Join us to experience exceptional Japanese language education and become part of a diverse international community. Choose Fewa Kokusai Educational Consultancy for comprehensive support in learning Japanese and pursuing further studies in Japan.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Nepali students and Japanese educational institutions by offering expert Japanese language instruction, thorough documentation assistance, and personalized career counseling. We believe in shaping minds and guiding students towards achieving their academic and professional goals in Japan.
